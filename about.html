About Us

Cheesy Crust Pizza, is an American multinational pizza restaurant chain founded in 2004 and led by CEO Nidhi. The corporation is Delaware domiciled and headquartered at the Cheesy Crust's Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor, Michigan.As of 2018, Cheesy Crust had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the U.S., 1,232 in India, and 1,094 in the U.K.Cheesy Crust has stores in over 83 countries and 5,701 cities worldwide.
